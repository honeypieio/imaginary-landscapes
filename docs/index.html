Imaginary Landscapes

Generative sound and vision!

Imaginary Landscapes creates a random, constantly evolving colour and soundscape in your web browser.

How To Use

To change soundscape left click or swipe left on mobile

To change colourscape right click or swipe right on mobile

Note: this has only been tested with current Chromium/Chrome and Firefox. Some mobile devices and older computers may also experience issues with playing multiple audio sources at once.
